How to Earn Your First Stripe

  1. Go to Civil Air Patrol eServices and create an account.
  2. Complete Operation Security Awareness Training (OPSEC).
    You will be directed to complete this training when you log in to eServices.

    3. Promotion Requirements

  3. Complete CAP Equal Opportunity Training (EO).
    You can complete this training here.
  4. Introduction to CAP Safety course completed in “Safety Management System.”
    Direct Link to Intro to CAP Safety Training.
  5. Online Leadership Test. Complete Learn to Lead Chapter 1 Online Leadership Test with 80% or higher.
    Cadet online testing can be completed under “Cadet Online Testing” on e-services or here.
  6. Drill & Ceremonies Practical Test.
    Pass Achievement 1 Drill & Ceremonies Practical Test (CAPT 78-2) with 73% (11/15) or higher.
    Request test here. Link to Score Sheet.
  7. Cadet Physical Fitness Test (CPFT). Pass fitness test according to the CAPP 52-18 Fitness Test Requirements.
    Cadets must pass either the mile run or shuttle run, and two of the other events.
    If you are injured or medically unable to participate in the CPFT, this requirement will be waived.
  8. Ground School Module.
    You will complete this module as a guided activity during a Squadron meeting. Once completed, your flight staff will turn in your discussion participation to the Deputy Commander for Cadets.
  9. Participate actively in CAP meetings and/or activities.
  10. Cadet Oath. Be able to recite the Cadet Oath from Memory.

    11. Squadron and Wing Requirements

  11. Uniforms. Possess and properly wear CAP Service and BDU Uniforms in accordance with CAPM 39-1.
  12. Basic Risk Management course completed in “Safety Management System."
    Direct Link to Intro to CAP Safety Training.

    13. Final Step!

  13. Review Board Request a review board here.
